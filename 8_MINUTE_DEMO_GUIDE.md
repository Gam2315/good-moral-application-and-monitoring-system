## üöÄ **STEP-BY-STEP DEMO FLOW**

### **OPENING (30 seconds)**
**"Good [morning/afternoon]! I'm presenting our Good Moral Application and Violations Monitoring System - a solution that transforms weeks of manual processing into days of automated efficiency."**

**Action**: Display welcome page
**Key Point**: "Traditional manual processes create bottlenecks. Our digital solution eliminates them."

---

### **SEGMENT 1: STUDENT EXPERIENCE (2 minutes)**

#### **Why Start Here**: Students are the primary users - show immediate value

**"Let me show you the student journey - the heart of our system."**

**Action 1** (30 seconds): Login as student
- **Show**: Clean, intuitive dashboard
- **Say**: "Students access a personalized portal where they can see their status at a glance."

**Action 2** (45 seconds): Apply for Good Moral Certificate
- **Show**: Application form with validation
- **Say**: "The application process is streamlined with real-time validation and clear instructions."
- **Navigate**: Fill key fields, upload document, submit

**Action 3** (45 seconds): Track application status
- **Show**: Status tracking with progress indicators
- **Say**: "Real-time tracking eliminates the need for repeated inquiries. Students know exactly where their application stands."
- **Highlight**: Timeline view, notification system

**Transition**: *"Now let's see how efficiently violations are managed on the administrative side."*

---

### **SEGMENT 2: VIOLATION MANAGEMENT (2 minutes)**

#### **Why This Order**: Show systematic approach to discipline management

**"Our violation management system ensures no incident goes untracked."**

**Action 1** (45 seconds): Login as Moderator, add violation
- **Show**: Violation entry form, student lookup
- **Say**: "Moderators can quickly add violations with automatic student lookup and classification."
- **Demonstrate**: Search student, select violation type, add details

**Action 2** (45 seconds): Show violation dashboard
- **Show**: Violation tracking, escalation alerts
- **Say**: "The system automatically tracks patterns and triggers escalations based on severity."
- **Highlight**: Minor vs major violations, automatic notifications

**Action 3** (30 seconds): View escalation workflow
- **Show**: Cases requiring Dean attention
- **Say**: "Major violations are automatically escalated to appropriate authorities."

**Transition**: *"This brings us to our streamlined approval process."*

---

### **SEGMENT 3: APPROVAL PROCESS (2 minutes)**

#### **Why Critical**: Shows decision support and workflow automation

**"Decision-makers get comprehensive information for informed approvals."**

**Action 1** (60 seconds): Login as Dean, review pending applications
- **Show**: Application queue with student summaries
- **Say**: "Deans see all pending applications with key student information at a glance."
- **Navigate**: Click on specific application

**Action 2** (60 seconds): Review student profile and approve
- **Show**: Comprehensive student profile with violation history
- **Say**: "Complete student background - academic standing, violation history, and application details - supports informed decision-making."
- **Action**: Approve application with comments
- **Highlight**: Approval workflow, automatic notifications

**Transition**: *"Let me show you the administrative intelligence that drives the system."*

---

### **SEGMENT 4: SYSTEM INTELLIGENCE (1 minute)**

#### **Why Powerful**: Demonstrates advanced features and insights

**"Our system provides institutional insights and automated monitoring."**

**Action 1** (30 seconds): Login as Admin, view dashboard
- **Show**: System-wide analytics, violation trends
- **Say**: "Administrators get real-time insights into institutional patterns and system performance."

**Action 2** (30 seconds): Show automated features
- **Show**: Escalation notifications, processing metrics
- **Say**: "Automated workflows ensure compliance while providing transparency through comprehensive reporting."
- **Highlight**: Certificate generation, notification system

---

### **CLOSING (30 seconds)**

**"The results speak for themselves: 75% reduction in processing time, 100% transparency for students, and streamlined workflows for administrators."**

**Final Screen**: Impact summary or system overview
**"This system transforms administrative efficiency while improving student experience. Questions?"**
